Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

I think progressive enhancement is important, because it makes sure that content and features are available for users with outdated technology. Applications with progressive enhancement are built and won't break as easily due to browser inconsistencies or missing features. By accommodating with a lot of devices and user contexts, progressive enhancement helps developers provide to a larger audience, even people in regions with less advanced tech.

As technology gets better, progressive enhancement helps that websites remain constant, even with specific features or APIs that aren't available.
